Phishing Awareness

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers impersonate legitimate entities to steal sensitive information.

How to Recognize Phishing

  • Check the sender's email address.
  • Look for spelling and grammatical errors.
  • Hover over links to see their actual URL.

What to Do If You Encounter Phishing

Do not click on links or provide personal information. Report the email to your IT department or email provider.
